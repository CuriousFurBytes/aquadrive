Toggles / Accordions
What wash packages do you offer?
We offer four tiers: Basic Exterior ($18), Interior + Exterior ($45), Premium Detail ($89), and Full Ceramic Coat ($249). Each package includes complimentary tire shine and window cleaning.
How long does a typical wash take?
A Basic Exterior wash takes approximately 20 minutes. Interior + Exterior runs about 45 minutes. Premium Detail needs around 90 minutes, and a Full Ceramic Coat requires approximately 3 hours.
Can I reschedule my booking?
Yes! You can reschedule any booking up to 2 hours before the appointment at no extra charge. Simply go to your booking history and tap "Reschedule" on the active booking.
Do you offer a loyalty program?
Absolutely. Every wash earns points toward free services. After 5 washes you unlock Loyalty status with 10% off, and after 10 washes you reach Premium tier with 15% off plus priority booking.
